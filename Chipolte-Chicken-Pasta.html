Creamy Chipolte Chicken Pasta

Description

Ingredients

  • Ingredients to Blend
    • 340ml Fat Free Evaporated Milk
    • 60g Chopped Parmesian Cheese
    • 2tbsps Chipolte Sauce
    • 90g Light Cream Cheese
  • Ingredients to Cook
    • 1.8lbs Raw Chicken Breast
    • 1 Pack Taco seasoning
    • 1 White/Brown Onion
    • 6 Garlic Cloves
    • 320g Protein Pasta
    • 1/2 Chickion Boullion Cube
    • 2tsp Smoke Paprika

Prep Instructions

  1. Things to Chop & Set Aside
    1. Full onion
    2. 6 Garlic Cloves
    3. Parmesian Cheese
    4. Chicken Breast(Season after chopping)
  2. Things to Measure+Blend
    1. 340ml Fat Free Evaporated Milk
    2. 60g Chopped Parmesian Cheese
    3. 2tbsps Chipolte Sauce
    4. 90g Light Cream Cheese
  3. Measure out 320g Pasta & Set aside
