Burger Quesadilla

Description

Shopping List

  • 165g lean ground beef(5%)
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • lettuce
  • Ketchup
  • Light Mayoanase
  • Mustard
  • Tortilla
  • Pickle

Cooking & Prep

  1. (Prep) To a bowl add:
    1. 1 tbsp light mayonnaise
    2. 2/3 tbsp ketchup
    3. 1 tsp mustard
    4. Mix together
  2. Other Prep
    1. Slice Lettuce
    2. Slice Pickles
    3. Save for later
  3. Cooking
    1. Roll mince into large ball & flatten it onto toritlla
    2. Fry on both sides to your liking
    3. While still frying and with mince side up, add cheese, then cover. Cook until cheese starts to melt
    4. Add sliced lettuce + pickles.
    5. Add Sauce
    6. Enjoy!
