Fudgy Choclate Protein Oats

Description

Shopping List

  • 45g rolled oats
  • 180g 0% fat Greek yogurt
  • 65ml milk
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 Tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 Tsp vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt

Cooking instructions

  • Add all measured out ingredients to a mason jar
  • Mix ingredients
  • Add to Mason Jar, top with Choc chips, leave in fridge over night!
